My Family Tree

A collection of memories and connections

Me

Here I am at the center of the tree.

Parents

Dad

My dad taught me how to ride a bike when I was 6. I fell a lot, but he always encouraged me to get back up.

Mom

Mom always made the best pancakes on Sunday mornings. We’d sit together and plan the week ahead.

Grandparents

Grandpa (Dad's side)

Grandpa used to tell stories from his childhood on the farm. He loved gardening and showed me how to plant tomatoes.

Grandma (Dad's side)

Grandma made amazing apple pies and taught me how to bake. Her kitchen always smelled like cinnamon.

Grandpa (Mom's side)

He was a quiet man who loved fishing. I remember sitting by the lake with him, learning patience.

Grandma (Mom's side)

She knitted me my first scarf, bright red and warm. She always told me to stay curious and kind.
